Reality Shows

Korean variety shows are the equivalent of reality shows, but they typically feature celebrities. They are typically showcasing the celebrities' normal side, offering a fascinating look into their life beyond the camera!


Chef And My Fridge Jinny's Kitchen Kian's Bizarre B&B
"Chef and My Fridge" is a cooking competition show where celebrity chefs compete to create dishes using only the ingredients found in celebrity guests' fridges. "Jinny's Kitchen" is a reality show where a group of celebrities run a Korean street food restaurant in a foreign country. "Kian's Bizarre B&B" is a reality show where webtoon artist Kian84 designs and runs a unique bed and breakfast on a remote island with the help of BTS's Jin and Ji Ye-eun.
Culinary Class Wars Knowing Bros Physical 100
"Culinary Class Wars" is a cooking competition where 100 chefs, divided into "Black Spoons" (undiscovered talents) and "White Spoons" (renowned chefs), compete for culinary excellence. Knowing Bros is a variety show where a group of male entertainers act as high school students, and celebrity guests are invited to be "transfer students". "Physical: 100" is a reality competition show where 100 physically fit individuals compete in a series of grueling challenges to determine who possesses the "perfect physique".
