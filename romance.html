Queen of Tears
Themes Summary Personal opinion Suggestion Rating
Romance, comedy, a little bit of violence The story centers on married couple Baek Hyun-woo and Hong Hae-in, the heiress to a wealthy company. Over the past three years of marriage, family pressure, work, and miscommunications has been adding tension in their relationship. As they navigate the obstacles and hardships that come their way, Hyun-woo and Hae-in begin to rediscover their love for each other and the reason they married in the first place. I personally loved Queen of Tears, and it kept me hooked until the end. The story was engaging, and all the characters were incredibly likeable. If you are looking for a heart-warming story about love and loyalty. 4/5
