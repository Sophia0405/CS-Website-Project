Romance/Rom-com

Korean rom-com shows are notorious for being overly clich√© and cheesy, but this adds to their charm. It is the perfect thing to watch when looking for a silly pick-me-up!


Business Proposal Crash Landing on You Queen of Tears
"Business Proposal" is about a woman, Shin Ha-ri, who disguises as her friend on a blind date to scare him away. But plans go awry when he turns out to be her CEO - and makes a proposal. "Crash Landing on You" follows Yoon Se-ri, a South Korean businesswoman and heiress, who crash-lands in North Korea after a paragliding accident and finds love in a North Korean army captain. "Queen of Tears" follows the marital journey of Baek Hyun-woo, a man from a humble background, and Hong Hae-in, a chaebol heiress and CEO of the Queens Department Store, as they navigate a crisis that threatens their marriage.
Crash Course in Romance Love Next Door Hometown Cha Cha Cha
"Crash Course in Romance" follows Haeng-seon, a banchan shop owner, as she navigates the cutthroat world of private education for her daughter while falling in love with her daughter's math instructor. "Love Next Door" follows the complex and humorous journey of childhood friends, Seok-ryu and Seung-hyo, who reconnect as adults after Seok-ryu returns to South Korea. "Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha" is a Korean drama series about Yoon Hye-jin, a big-city dentist, who moves to a seaside village and meets the charming, jack-of-all-trades Hong Du-sik.
